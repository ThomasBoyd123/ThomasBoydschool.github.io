Bend

Bend is a city in Oregon most known for it's outdoor sports and scenic nature spots. Bend is also the largest city in the central Oregon area despite only having a population of 100,421. This is most likely because of the small density of most cities in central Oregon as well as Oregon being the state in the U.S with the highest amount of "Ghost Towns". Bend has a typical climate similar to high deserst with cool nights and sunny days. Bend's outdoor living style is also accompanied by there being many lodges and hotels that focus on these points.
